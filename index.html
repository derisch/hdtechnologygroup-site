01 · What we do

HD Technology Group LLC is a Michigan-based software company building inventory management tools for physical goods. Our first product provides storage-location tracking, movement history, and cost-basis accounting for sealed trading card product, using market pricing data to value holdings.

A

Location tracking

Every unit is tied to a storage container and location, so you always know where a given item physically sits.

B

Movement history

A full audit trail of moves between locations — what went where, and when — recorded as it happens.

C

Cost-basis accounting

Purchase costs and acquisition dates on record, valued against current market pricing data to give a true picture of holdings.
